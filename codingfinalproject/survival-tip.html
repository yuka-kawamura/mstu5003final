Survival Tips

1.Wifi

In Japan, there are not many free wifi spot available. It is reccomended to buy sim card or rent mobile wifi at the airport.

2.Bring cash

Japan is still a very cash heavy country. A lot of people use cash. Although most of the places accept credit card, it is reccomended to bring enough cash.

3.Useful app

There are some must have apps to travel in Japan. First, GuruNavi is an app which is very similar to Yelp. It introudces popular places to eat around Japan.

NaviTokyo, is an app to navigate transpotations in Tokyo. The transportaion can be complicated to follow. This will be a helpful app to get to the most effecient, cheap, easy transportaion.
